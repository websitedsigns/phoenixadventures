Embark on a Thrilling Adventure

Travel through Europe on a luxury sleeper coach and experience the excitement of visiting multiple theme parks. Ride some of the best coasters and enjoy an unforgettable journey filled with adrenaline, fun, and memories to last a lifetime!

Useful Info

The recommended age is 16+ but children aged 3+ are accepted when accompanied by an appropriate adult. Children must be able to sleep unaccompanied in a bunk.

Experience multiple thrilling parks over multiple days – from family favourites to Europe’s biggest roller coasters!

Travel in style on a VIP luxury sleeper coach with bunk beds, comfy couches, kitchen facilities, and entertainment.

Rest in private, comfortable bunks with soft bedding as you travel overnight between parks.

Tickets are not included – this allows flexibility for annual pass holders. Packages will be available approx 8 weeks prior to travel if required.

Enjoy the ultimate coach experience – sleep in your own bunk, wake up at a new park, socialise in lounges, and travel like a celebrity on a band bus.
