Useful Information

Here you will find all the information you will need.

Traveling on a sleeper coach to visit five of the best theme parks in Europe offers a convenient and efficient way to experience all the thrill and excitement these attractions have to offer. With comfortable sleeping accommodations and onboard amenities, passengers can relax and unwind during their journey, arriving refreshed and ready to enjoy a full day of fun at each park.

From adrenaline-pumping roller coasters to immersive themed lands, visitors can explore a diverse range of attractions, ensuring there is something for everyone to enjoy. By travelling on a sleeper coach, guests also have the added benefit of saving time and money on accommodation costs, allowing them to maximise their time at each park without worrying about finding nearby lodging.

Overall, embarking on this adventure offers a unique and memorable way to experience some of the top theme parks in Europe while enjoying the comfort and convenience of travelling by sleeper coach.

Trip consists of:

  • Sleeper coach travel from your chosen boarding point to each park on the itinerary.
  • 1 full day to spend in each park: Toverland, Efteling, Movie Park, Phantasialand & Plopsaland Belgium
  • Spend each night of the trip on the sleeper coach with your own private bunk.
  • Return Ferry crossing from Dover to Calais
